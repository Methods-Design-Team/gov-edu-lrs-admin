Reporting a data problem

If you need to report a problem with a learner’s Unique Learner Number (ULN) or Personal Learner Record (PLR) it’s best to do this through the portal, as any data you include will be sent securely.

You can do this from either the ‘Profile’ or ‘Personal learning record’ tab on an individual’s learner profile.

For any other queries

You can email the LRS service desk at:

LRS.Support@education.gov.uk

If you do need to email us, you must ensure any personal data is sent securely. There’s more information about encrypting emails in our data encryption and passphrase guidance.
